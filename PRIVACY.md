# Privacy Policy

CuteSpace is a local Windows desktop app.

## What The App Stores

CuteSpace may store the following data on your PC:

- Modes and launch items you create.
- Shortcuts and routine settings.
- Clipboard history captured by the app.
- App preferences such as language, style, and startup behavior.
- Local error logs used for troubleshooting.

By default, app data is stored under:

```text
%AppData%\CuteSpace\
```

## What The App Uploads

CuteSpace does not upload your data.

The app does not send your modes, shortcuts, clipboard history, settings, or logs to a server. There are no analytics, telemetry, cloud sync, or external accounts in this version.

## Network Use

CuteSpace can open websites that you configure as shortcuts or mode items. Those websites are opened in your browser. CuteSpace itself does not transmit your local app data.

## Removing Your Data

To remove local CuteSpace data, close the app and delete:

```text
%AppData%\CuteSpace\
```

You can also disable clipboard history or clear entries from inside the app where available.
